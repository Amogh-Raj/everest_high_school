Principal's Message

Welcome to Our School, Shaping and Nurturing Minds

Dear Parents and Students,

Welcome to Everest High School. As the Principal, I am honored to lead an institution that has been shaping young minds and building future leaders for over two decades.

At Everest High School, we believe that every child has unique talents and abilities. Our dedicated team of educators works tirelessly to create an environment where these talents can flourish. We focus not just on academic excellence, but also on character development and life skills.

Our commitment to providing quality education is unwavering, and we continuously strive to adapt our teaching methods to meet the changing needs of our students in this dynamic world.

I believe that quality education is the key for a better society and that build takes a lot of time and patience. I believe in a greater society where minds of equal strength come together to make something better everyday.

I look forward to working together with our school community to create bright futures for our students.

Ms. Rajani Raj
Principal, Everest High School
