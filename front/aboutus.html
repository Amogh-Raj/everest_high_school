About Us

Excellence Through Education

Our Story

Founded in 1999, Everest High School has been a beacon of educational excellence for over 25 years. We provide comprehensive and deep education from Nursery to Grade 10, focusing on developing well-rounded individuals who are capable not only in class, but also out in the world.

Our Vision

To create a learning environment where every student can discover their potential and develop into responsible global citizens who are emphathetic, available and compassionate.

Our Mission

We strive to provide quality education through innovative teaching methods, modern facilities, and a nurturing environment that encourages both academic excellence and personal growth. We also strive to achive excellence through intellectual debates and knowledge sharing.
